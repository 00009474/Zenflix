Movies and Serials

Stranger Things (2016-...)
American science fiction series created by the Duffer brothers for the streaming service Netflix. The series is set in the 1980s in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana. Brave guys will have to learn the terrible secrets of their hometown
Breaking Bad (2008-2013)
The story of Walter White, a schoolteacher who was diagnosed with inoperable lung cancer. Together with his former student Jesse Pinkman, he begins manufacturing and marketing methamphetamine to secure the financial future of his family.
Game of Thrones (2011-2019)
The story of a medieval country's civil war. The series is set on the fictional continents of Westeros and Essos in a world where seasons stretch on for years. Follow the interesting storyline and impossible U-turns that happened in Westeros
Dexter (2006-2013)
By day, mild-mannered Dexter is a blood-spatter analyst for the Miami police. But at night, he is a serial killer who only targets other murderers. As Dexter himself calls him "Dark fellow traveler". Find out what happened in his life and changed him forever
Californication (2007-2014)
Humor, sex and charisma - David Duchovny as a misanthrope writer tries to get out of the creative crisis Trying to get out of the hole into which the writer started himself, he tries to quit drugs and alcohol and restore relations with his ex-wife and daughter.
Rick and Morty (2013-....)
The funny adventures of the alcoholic Rick and his grandson Morty seem at first glance to be a banal fiction, poured back into the future. However, everything is not so simple, have you ever heard of the wub0ba lubba dub dub? To be honest, neither are we.
Fargo (2014-....)
The show is inspired by the eponymous 1996 film, which was written and directed by the Coen brothers. The Coens were impressed by Hawley's script and agreed to be named as executive producers. Each season features a new and different story that reflects America at the time.
Black Mirror (2011-....)
The sci-fi series reflects the familiar world that is shrouded in technology. But is everything so rosy? Each episode is a separate plot showing what a modern technological development process can bring. Start your browsing now and see how the usual start of a wired world ends
True Detective (2014-....)
Seasonal anthology series in which police investigations unearth the personal and professional secrets of those involved, both within and outside the law. Each season reveals detectives' desperate struggle with new crimes, revealing their undisclosed sides of personality
