HBO approves filming of the full first season of the film adaptation of The Last of Us

Last spring, HBO launched the film adaptation of Naughty Dog's hit hit The Last of Us, which brought the world to its cult series Uncharted. Since then, screenwriter Craig Mazin (Chernobyl) and creative director of the original source Neil Druckmann have worked on the project. And today we can say with confidence that their work was crowned with success: having familiarized with their creative work, the channel gave the go-ahead for the filming of the full first season.
